Wellington City Council

Wellington City Council was founded 1870. Wellington City Council is a territorial authority in New Zealand, governing the city of Wellington, the country's capital city and third-largest city by population.

Water Meter

Water Meter measure water usage in residential and commercial building units supplied by public water systems.

Sluice Valve

Sluice Valve is installed to control the flow of water through a straight pipe and as a shut off valve to isolate supply. The water utlity can use it to isolate sections of water supply for maintenance or to shut of majot leaks.

Sightseeing

Old Bank

The Old Bank Building is one of Wellington's most famous landmarks. 2001 marks the hundred-year anniversary of this magnificent building. It was build between 1883 and 1904, designed by Thomas Turnbull. It operated a branch on the ground floor from 1901 and 1984.

Museum of New Zealand

The Museum of New Zealand Te Papa Tongarewa is New Zealand's national museum. Known as Te Papa, it opened since 1998, more than 1.5 million people visit here every year.

Weta Workshop

Weta Workshop was founded in 1987. One of the world's premier visual effects studios, Weta Digital is known for its Academy Award-winning visual effects for such films as The Lord of the Rings, King Kong, and Avatar.
